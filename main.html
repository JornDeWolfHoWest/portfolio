MayHamster

Currently in development! More to come!

4 player game where three players control a hamster and one player controls a human. The goal for the hamsters is to kill themselves in any way nessecary. The human must prevent hamsters from dying!


I worked on:
- The player movement
- Round system
- The camera system that switches the cameras for the players, so you keep looking at one spot while you switch to a human or hamster
- Worked on the window death mechanic
- Body system
- Points system.
- UI implementation
- Notification system to let the human know what is turned on/off
